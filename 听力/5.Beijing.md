Beijing is the capital city of china, it has a long history and many famous landmarks, like the great wall, the forbidden city and the temple of heaven

People in Beijing enjoy the delicious peking duck and practice traditional Chinese arts, like calligraphy(书法) and martial(军事的) arts.

This city is bustling(混合混杂) with modern buildings and ancient temples, creating a unique blend(混合) of old and new.

Visitors can experience the rich culture and history of china in Beijing.
